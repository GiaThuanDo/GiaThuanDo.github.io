Research & Development

Hello! I'm a second-year master's student at the Graduate Institute of Economics, National Taipei University. I'm particularly interested in microeconomics. My thesis, titled "On the Indirect Emissions in Upstream-Downstream Markets," falls under the field of industrial organization.

It explores how indirect emissions influence the decision-making of upstream and downstream firms, and how these effects relate to environmental corporate social responsibility (ECSR) and social welfare.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I'm passionate about leveraging technology and economic principles to address real-world challenges, particularly in sustainability and environmental economics.
